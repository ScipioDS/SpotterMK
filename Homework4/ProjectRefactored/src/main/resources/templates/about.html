Welcome to SpotterMK, your go-to platform for data-driven insights into the Macedonian Stock Exchange. Developed by three passionate students from the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (FINKI) at UKIM - Skopje, this app combines innovation and expertise to revolutionize how investors approach the stock market.

Our team has harnessed 10 years of historical stock data, real-time updates, and advanced machine learning algorithms to create a platform that delivers accurate predictions on stock movements. What sets us apart is our integration of sentiment analysis from market-relevant news stories, providing a holistic view of the factors influencing stock prices.

Whether you're a seasoned investor analyzing trends, a portfolio manager mitigating risks, or a newcomer making your first investment, SpotterMK offers the tools and insights to help you succeed.

Built by students, designed for the future. Join us as we bring smarter investing to everyone.
